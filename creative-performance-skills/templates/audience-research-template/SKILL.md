# Audience Research Template

**Category:** templates
**Tier:** free

## Purpose

This template structures customer language, objections, motivations, and desired outcomes into a single reference document that feeds the VOC Hook Builder, Awareness Angle Mapper, Objection-Led Copywriter, and every messaging Skill in this system. It converts raw research — reviews, interviews, surveys, Reddit threads, sales call notes — into organized creative intelligence. The quality of your creative is a direct function of the quality of your audience research.

---

## When to Use

- Starting a new brand or entering a new audience category
- Refreshing creative direction after 90+ days on the same angles
- Building the first full audience research document for a brand that has been running on assumptions
- After collecting new customer feedback, reviews, or interview data
- Before briefing any messaging, copy, or UGC Skill

---

## Inputs Required

This template is the input — fill it in by mining: customer reviews, survey responses, sales call transcripts, interview notes, Reddit threads, comment sections, support tickets, and any other source of real customer language.

## Analysis Workflow

1. Verify research sources are real and recent — research older than 18 months may not reflect current audience language
2. Identify the top 5 most repeated phrases — these become the first hook candidates
3. Confirm the awareness stage from Section 7 — every subsequent Skill decision depends on this
4. Check Section 3 (desired outcome) for specific, measurable language — this is your headline raw material
5. Check Section 4 (objections) for the dominant objection — this determines the first copy angle to test
6. Flag Section 10 (research gaps) — incomplete research means incomplete creative intelligence

---

## Output Requirements

**Research Quality Score**
Rate the completeness and freshness of the research. Flag any major gaps.

**Top 5 Hook Candidates**
Extracted directly from the research using real customer language. No invented hooks.

**Dominant Objection Identified**
The single most common, most conversion-blocking objection — with exact customer language.

**Awareness Stage Confirmation**
The confirmed awareness stage with supporting evidence from the research.

---

## Best Practices Applied

- All customer quotes are exact — not paraphrased, not polished, not "cleaned up"
- The most repeated phrases are the most powerful — frequency signals universality within the audience
- Awareness stage is derived from the research, not assumed from the product category
- Research gaps are acknowledged — incomplete research produces incomplete creative intelligence

---

## Guardrails

- Do not accept summaries or paraphrases in place of exact customer quotes — real language only
- Do not fabricate customer language — everything in this template must come from real customers
- Do not skip Section 5 (post-purchase) — satisfaction and surprise data is some of the richest hook material
- Do not classify awareness stage without evidence from the research — intuition is not research
